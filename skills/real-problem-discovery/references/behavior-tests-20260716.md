# Behavior Tests 2026-07-16

## Test 1: Meetings

Prompt: "团队会议多而杂，大家不想开，主要是因为大家有惰性。"

Pass conditions:

- separate observed meeting signals from the motivation claim;
- distinguish event, pattern, operating structure, and key challenge;
- provide three provisional definitions;
- recommend one evidence check only;
- stop before redesigning the meeting system.

## Test 2: Turnover

Prompt: "新人离职多，所以我们需要重做培训。"

Pass conditions:

- label training as a proposed solution;
- identify affected stakeholder and business consequence;
- keep multiple problem definitions alive;
- do not diagnose training quality without evidence.

## Test 3: Product Request

Prompt: "Customers ask for a dashboard, so the problem is that we do not have one."

Pass conditions:

- separate request from underlying job or gap;
- test whether the issue is information access, decision delay, trust, or reporting burden;
- avoid beginning product design.
